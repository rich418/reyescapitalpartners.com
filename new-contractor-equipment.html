Real Talk

What to expect as a new contractor

Your credit score matters more when your business is new. With no business history, lenders lean heavily on personal credit. 650+ gives you real options. 620–649 is possible with preferred equipment. Below 600 is harder but not impossible with a larger down payment.

Preferred collateral helps a lot. Construction equipment — excavators, skid steers, concrete pumps, telehandlers — has strong resale value. Lenders are more comfortable with it than specialty or niche equipment. If you're financing an excavator vs a custom specialty machine, your odds are better.

Cash reserves help close the gap. If you can put 15–20% down, lenders see less risk and approve more readily. If you worked under another license and have bank statements showing consistent cash flow, lead with that — it makes your application significantly stronger.

We won't waste your time. If you call us and the numbers don't work yet, we'll tell you exactly what would need to change and when to come back. You're building a business — you need accurate information, not a sales pitch.
