Tax Strategy

The Section 179 advantage

Most contractors don't know about this. The ones who do use it every year to put equipment on the job site and keep cash in their pocket.

Finance equipment.
Deduct 100% this year.

Section 179 lets you deduct the full purchase price of qualifying equipment in the year it's placed in service — even if you financed it. You don't need to pay cash to get the full deduction.

2025 federal deduction limit: $1,220,000

Equipment financed$80,000
Section 179 deduction$80,000
Business profit this year$120,000
Taxable income after deduction$40,000
Tax bill saved (35% bracket)~$28,000
Instead of writing a check to IRS — you own equipment that makes you money✓

* Consult your CPA. General illustration only. CA Section 179 capped at $25,000 state deduction.

Common Questions

Everything you want to know

Straight answers from contractors and business owners we hear every day.

What credit score do I need to qualify?+
We work with lenders approving scores from 550+. Higher scores get better rates, but we have programs for challenged credit, recent bankruptcies, and startups. Best way to know is to apply — no cost, no obligation.
How long does approval take?+
Most decisions come back within 24 hours. Deals under $75,000 can get approved within a few hours. Once approved and docs are signed, funding is typically same day or next business day.
What documents do I need to apply?+
For most deals under $75,000 we only need a completed application and equipment invoice — no financials required. Larger deals may require bank statements or tax returns. We keep it simple.
Can I finance used equipment?+
Yes — we finance new and used. Used equipment is common in construction and our lenders are comfortable with it. Age and condition may affect terms but rarely disqualifies a deal.
Is my business too new to qualify?+
Not necessarily. We have lenders for startups and businesses under 2 years old. May require a down payment or personal guarantee, but options exist. We'll be honest about what to expect.
Do you finance dental, medical, or restaurant equipment?+
Yes — dental chairs, CAD/CAM systems, commercial kitchen equipment, veterinary equipment, solar systems, and more. Any business equipment that generates revenue qualifies. Call us if you're not sure about your specific equipment type.
What are the minimum and maximum amounts?+
Minimum is $5,000. We can finance $500,000+ depending on profile and lender. Most deals range from $15,000 to $250,000.
Does it cost anything to apply?+
No — applying is completely free. We only get paid when your deal funds. Our fee is built into the financing so there are no out-of-pocket costs to you for our services.
Can you help me find the equipment too?+
Yes — that's our white glove service. Tell us what you need and we'll source options from verified California dealers, put together a bilingual pricing report with Section 179 savings, and then finance the deal once you pick. One call handles everything.
