What credit score do I need to finance a skid steer?+
590+ for businesses over 2 years. 650+ for new businesses. Skid steers are preferred collateral which helps — lenders are more flexible on credit for this equipment type.
Can I finance a used Bobcat?+
Yes — used skid steers 2015 and newer qualify with most programs, no down payment required. Earlier models can still be financed but may require 10–20% down depending on age and condition.
How much is a typical monthly payment on a skid steer?+
A $55,000 Bobcat at 12% APR over 60 months is approximately $1,222/month. A $35,000 used Caterpillar at 18% over 48 months is approximately $1,030/month. Use our calculator at reyescapitalpartners.com.
Can a brand new landscaping company finance a skid steer?+
Yes — skid steers are one of the best equipment types for new businesses. Typically requires 650+ FICO and one advance payment. No financials needed for deals under $75K.
What's the difference between a skid steer and compact track loader for financing?+
Same financing programs apply to both. Compact track loaders (rubber tracks) and wheeled skid steers are treated identically by our lenders. Both are preferred collateral.
